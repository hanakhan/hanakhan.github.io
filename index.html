GIS/Remote Sensing Projects

Geospatial Analysis using GIS and ENVI

  • Goal: visualize data of gray fox captures in the SF Bay Area marshes from 1999-2013 and identify which ponds are threatened by future sea level rise
  • Used QGIS and ENVI Classic to calculate the NDVI and change detection on Landsat satellite imagery to identify changes in vegetation in the marsh regions
  • Shared my findings with the Don Edwards National Refuge to identify ponds to prioritize for restoration and protection

   

Vegetation Change Detection

  • Goal: to test the hypothesis that mangroves in Bangladesh have been declining
  • Processed and manipulated Landsat satellite imagery on ENVI 5.0 using remote sensing algorithms, such as NDVI and change detection
  • Produced 3D surface view using infrared imagery and topography
  • Detailed my research process and findings on a blog
